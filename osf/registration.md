# Preregistration & deviations

- **Registration:** *Triple Replication Study: Impact of Brand Equity on the
  Resources of Nonprofit Organisations in Austria*, Open Science Framework.
- **Type:** pre-analysis, **post-survey** registration (data collected
  May–July 2025; registered before analysis).
- **Scope:** governs the *replication* of a single higher-order path
  (Brand Equity → Donation Intention). This paper reuses the same data for a
  measurement-defensibility comparison and is a **largely exploratory re-framing**.
- **Review link (anonymised, view-only):**
  `https://osf.io/ygt5n/overview?view_only=a474ebcfa09e471ab252d5475fc270dd`
- **Public link:** add on acceptance (end the embargo, then cite `osf.io/ygt5n`).

Deviations from the registered plan are documented in
`analysis/02_supplements_addendum.qmd` (§ Deviations) following
Bekkers et al. (2025).
